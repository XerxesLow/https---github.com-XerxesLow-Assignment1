About

Minatozaki Sana (湊崎 紗夏; born Dec 29, 1996), known mononymously as Sana, is a Japanese singer and a member of the Kpop group Twice formed by JYP Entertainment through the survival show Sixteen. Sana made her debut with Twice on Oct 20, 2015. Profile
