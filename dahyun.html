About

Kim Da Hyun (김다현; born May 28, 1998), simply known as Dahyun, is a South Korean singer and a member of the Kpop group Twice formed by JYP Entertainment through the reality show Sixteen. Dahyun made her debut with Twice on Oct 20, 2015. Profile
