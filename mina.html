About

Myōi Mina (名井 南; born Mar 24, 1997), simply known as Mina, is a Japanese singer and a member of the Kpop group Twice formed by JYP Entertainment through the survival show Sixteen. Mina made her debut with Twice on Oct 20, 2015. Profile
