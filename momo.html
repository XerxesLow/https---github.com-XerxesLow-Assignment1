About

Hirai Momo (平井 もも; born Nov 9, 1996), simply known as Momo, is a Japanese singer, dancer, and rapper based in South Korea. She is a member of the Kpop group Twice formed by JYP Entertainment through the survival show Sixteen. Momo made her debut with Twice on Oct 20, 2015. Profile
