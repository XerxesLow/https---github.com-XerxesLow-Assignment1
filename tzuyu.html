About

Chou Tzuyu (周子瑜; born Jun 14, 1999), simply known as Tzuyu, is a Taiwanese singer based in South Korea. She is the youngest member of the Kpop group Twice formed by JYP Entertainment through the reality show Sixteen. Tzuyu made her debut with Twice on Oct 20, 2015. Profile

  • Full Name: Chou Tzuyu
  • Country of Birth: Taiwan

  • Height: 170 cm (5'7'')
  • Weight: 48 kg (106 lbs)
  • Blood Type: A

    • Fun Facts and Trivia

  • Age trivia: Tzuyu was 16 years old (international age) when she debuted with Twice in 2015. Tzuyu is the youngest member (maknae) of Twice.
  • Height trivia: Tzuyu is the tallest member of Twice with a height of 170 cm (5'7''). She shares the same height as Yeji (Itzy), Sooyoung (SNSD), and Léa (Secret Number).
  • Tzuyu is the only Taiwan-born member from Twice. In fact, she is one of the few Taiwanese idols in the kpop industry. Other Taiwanese idols include: YangYang (NCT), Shuhua (from (G)I-DLE), and SoSo (GWSN).
