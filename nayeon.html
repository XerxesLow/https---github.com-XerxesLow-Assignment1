About

Im Nayeon (임나연; born Sep 22, 1995) is a South Korean singer and the oldest member of the Kpop group Twice under JYP Entertainment. Nayeon made her debut with Twice on Oct 20, 2015. Profile
  • Stage Name: Nayeon
  • Full Name: Im Na Yeon
  • Korean Name: 임나연
  • Country of Birth: South Korea
  • Birthday: Sep 22, 1995
  • Height: 164 cm (5'5'')
  • Weight: 47kg (104 lbs)
  • Blood Type: A
