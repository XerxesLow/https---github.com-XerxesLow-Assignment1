About

Yoo Jeong Yeon (유정연; born Nov 1, 1996), simply known as Jeongyeon, is a South Korean singer and a member of the Kpop group Twice formed by JYP Entertainment through the reality show Sixteen. Jeongyeon made her debut with Twice on Oct 20, 2015. Profile
